Computer Recycling

At Freddie's Computer Repair, we believe in doing our part to reduce e-waste and protect the environment. That's why we offer free computer recycling services and repurpose components whenever possible. For batteries, we charge a small fee of $20 per battery to cover the cost of safe disposal and recycling. Let us help you dispose of your old electronics responsibly and sustainably.
